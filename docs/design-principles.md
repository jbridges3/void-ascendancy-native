# Design Principles

These principles protect `Void Ascendancy Native` from drifting away from its identity during redesign and implementation.

## Preserve The Original Truth

The redesign should preserve the narrative sparks, systemic structure, and hidden-information philosophy of the terminal original.

## Interface Is Fiction

UI is not a neutral wrapper around game systems. It is part of the world and should communicate posture, ideology, and pressure.

## Native First

The app should feel like a real macOS application in structure, responsiveness, and usability. Fictional expression should live inside that clarity rather than replacing it.

## Restraint Over Maximalism

The app should not collapse into noisy cyberpunk styling, decorative glitch, or dashboard clutter. Meaningful selective effects are stronger than constant intensity.

## Place, Pressure, Resonance

Visual presentation should separate:

- place
- ideological pressure
- player resonance

This keeps colony identity, convergence, and alignment from collapsing into one flat visual treatment.

## Contradiction Over Simplification

Contested systems should feel contradictory, not merely blended. Elpis and Void should feel philosophically and aesthetically distinct.

## Legibility Is Sacred

The app may feel invasive or unstable, but it must remain fair, readable, and trustworthy at the interaction layer.

## Build The Vertical Slice To Prove The Thesis

A small playable sequence should prove the product vision before the project expands. Every system and presentation rule should support that goal.
