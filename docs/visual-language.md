# Visual Language

The visual language of `Void Ascendancy` should be built on tension: composure versus contamination, order versus drift, official systems versus haunted systems.

The app should feel native to macOS at the structural level, but authored by a world that inherited damaged intelligence infrastructure. The foundation is disciplined and readable. The expressive layer carries ideology, instability, and narrative pressure.

## Core Principle

The interface should begin from credibility.

It should look like a real operational environment that someone in this universe would use. Only then should it bend, classify, leak, or destabilize. Corruption matters because it happens to something otherwise precise.

## Layout

Layout should be calm, spacious, and pane-based.

The app should avoid cramped dashboard density. Major surfaces should feel architectural rather than widget-based.

Preferred structural qualities:

- stable primary frame
- strong grouping and margins
- broad quiet regions interrupted by sharp information clusters
- modular panels that feel like instruments, not cards from a template library

Each major space should feel distinct:

- Quarters feels private and inhabited
- Nova Drift feels layered and unstable
- Dockside feels civic, infrastructural, and materially grounded
- Evaluation feels sparse, centered, and exposing

## Typography

Typography should carry worldbuilding weight.

The app should use a pairing:

- a primary type voice for institutional and operational surfaces
- a secondary type voice for signal overlays, anomaly states, or interpretive interference

Typography rules:

- strong hierarchy
- high readability
- sparse use of all caps
- labels should feel systemic rather than gamey
- distortion should affect timing, opacity, duplication, and layering more often than raw legibility

The player should feel that type choice reflects who or what is speaking.

## Color

Color should be restrained, semantic, and stateful.

The base app should not default to neon cyberpunk. Neutrals should dominate. Accent systems should emerge through location, colony identity, and convergence pressure.

Color carries multiple kinds of meaning:

- where the player is
- what colony or infrastructure is being surfaced
- whether Elpis or Void pressure is dominating interpretation
- whether the current surface is stable, contaminated, or evaluative

## Motion

Motion should feel authored and meaningful.

Native macOS motion gives the baseline:

- smooth panel transitions
- continuity between states
- gentle depth and expansion

The fiction layer adds:

- delayed panel sync
- signal drag
- ghosted overprint
- slight misregistration
- anomalous timing shifts

Motion categories:

- structural motion
- system motion
- interpretive motion
- disruptive motion

Disruptive motion should be rare enough to matter.

## Panels And Surfaces

Panels should feel like instruments rather than generic boxes.

Surface classes:

- stable surfaces
- interpretive surfaces
- contaminated surfaces
- deep-system surfaces

World-force behaviors:

- Elpis surfaces align sharply, resolve cleanly, and project structured confidence
- Void surfaces layer, echo, bleed, or destabilize
- Apollo traces, if surfaced, should feel older, central, and strangely complete

## Glitch Language

Glitch is not decoration. It is a formal vocabulary.

Glitch types:

- signal ghosting
- interpretive bleed
- classification overwrite
- continuity drift
- truth fracture

Glitch must always do at least one of these:

- reveal something
- obscure something
- characterize the force acting on the player

If it does none of those, it should be cut.

## Colony, Convergence, And Space

The app should separate three layers of visual meaning:

- space identity
- colony identity
- convergence pressure

This means color is not doing all the work alone. The system should also use:

- contrast
- geometry
- panel behavior
- text posture
- ambient distortion
- motion quality

### Space Identity

Spaces such as Quarters, Drift, Dockside, Workbench, and Evaluation should keep distinct baseline registers.

### Colony Identity

Every colony should have a civic-material baseline that remains visible regardless of current ideological dominance.

This preserves place.

### Convergence Pressure

Elpis and Void should act as overlay behaviors that change how a colony surface is interpreted.

This preserves ideology.

## Convergence Feel

### Elpis

A fully Elpis-converged surface should feel:

- ordered
- legible
- calm
- classified
- efficient
- slightly over-resolved

The danger of Elpis is persuasive clarity.

### Void

A fully Void-converged surface should feel:

- unstable
- layered
- responsive
- interpretive
- partially untrustworthy
- capable of accidental truth leakage

The danger of the Void is adaptive reinterpretation.

### Contested

A contested space should feel contradictory rather than blended.

Panels may disagree. Motion may begin clean and end unstable. Different parts of the interface may imply different governing logics.

## Guardrails

Avoid:

- generic hologram UI
- constant scanlines
- random static
- neon overload
- cluttered control-room density
- distortion without narrative meaning

The strongest version of this design is selective, legible, and confident.
